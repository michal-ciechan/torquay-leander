# About Us

**Sub-pages:** [Our Squads](our-squads.md) | [Our Facilities](our-facilities.md) | [Membership](membership.md) | [Join Us](join-us.md) | [Codes of Conduct](codes-of-conduct.md)

Torquay Leander Swimming Club is a friendly, ambitious, and community-focused swimming club based in the heart of Torquay. We are dedicated to helping swimmers of all ages and abilities achieve their full potential, whether they are swimming for fitness, personal development, or competitive success.

With a strong emphasis on quality coaching, athlete development, and team spirit, we provide a safe and supportive environment where swimmers can grow in confidence both in and out of the pool. Our structured training pathway allows swimmers to progress at their own pace, supported by experienced coaches who are passionate about developing technique, performance, and a lifelong love of swimming.

At Torquay Leander, we pride ourselves on being more than just a swimming club. We are a welcoming community where friendships are built, achievements are celebrated, and every swimmer is encouraged to be the very best version of themselves.
