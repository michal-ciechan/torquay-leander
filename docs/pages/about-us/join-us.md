# Join Us

Whether your goal is to improve fitness, compete at a higher level, or simply enjoy swimming as part of a great community, Torquay Leander Swimming Club welcomes you.

We are always excited to hear from new swimmers and families interested in becoming part of the club.

**Ready to Dive In?**

Interested in coming for a free trial? Please register your interest below. We understand it's not just about your ability but also the environment you train in — feeling comfortable with your fellow swimmers, coaches and surroundings is the best way to reach your potential.

## The Torquay Leander Difference

- No minimum requirements for hours of training to be selected for squads
- We understand life is busy! Swim the hours you have available across sessions throughout the week. Our competing swimmers are chosen on merit, ability and attitude — not the number of hours you commit to swim.
- Professional qualified coaching for every training session
- Specific annual training plan built around our Gala program. Every session is designed with the next gala in mind ensuring optimal training volume and intensity.
- Competitive squad pathway from learn-to-race in our Development Squad, Junior Squad and through to elite swimmers in our Senior Squad
- Friendly, inclusive, and family-focused atmosphere with high membership retention across all age groups
- Opportunities to compete locally, regionally, and nationally
- Focus on fitness, discipline, teamwork, and personal development
