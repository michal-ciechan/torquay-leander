# Our Coaches

All of our coaches are volunteers who give up their own time to support, encourage, and develop every swimmer in the club. They are not paid for the countless hours they dedicate poolside, at competitions, and behind the scenes — they do it because they genuinely care about the swimmers and want to help each individual achieve their potential.

Our coaches are passionate about creating a positive environment where swimmers can learn, improve, build confidence, and enjoy the sport. Their commitment goes far beyond teaching technique; they provide encouragement, guidance, and support to help swimmers grow both in and out of the pool.

Quite simply, if they did not care, they would not continue to give their personal time so generously. We ask that all members, swimmers, and parents show our coaching team the same respect, support, and appreciation that they consistently show to our swimmers every day.
