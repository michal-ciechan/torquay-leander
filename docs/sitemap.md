### Welcome to Torquay Leander Swimming Club

**Building Confidence, Fitness & Champions in the Water**

Founded with a passion for swimming excellence and community spirit, Torquay Leander Swimming Club is one of Devon's leading competitive swimming clubs. Based in the beautiful coastal town of Torquay, we provide a supportive and inspiring environment for swimmers to reach their potential.

We coach swimming from swimmers stage 5 upwards, continuing to develop confidence, refine technique, stamina and performance to not only county but also regional and national level.

Our experienced coaching team is dedicated to helping every swimmer reach their full potential through structured training, skill development, and a positive team culture.

### The Torquay Leander Difference

- No minimum requirements for hours of training to be selected for squads
- We understand life is busy! Swim the hours you have available across sessions throughout the week. Our competing swimmers are chosen on merit, ability and attitude — not the number of hours you commit to swim.
- Professional qualified coaching for every training session
- Specific annual training plan built around our Gala program. Every session is designed with the next gala in mind ensuring optimal training volume and intensity.
- Competitive squad pathway from learn-to-race in our Development Squad, Junior Squad and through to elite swimmers in our Senior Squad
- Friendly, inclusive, and family-focused atmosphere with high membership retention across all age groups
- Opportunities to compete locally, regionally, and nationally
- Focus on fitness, discipline, teamwork, and personal development

### More Than Just a Swimming Club

At Torquay Leander, we pride ourselves on creating a welcoming community where swimmers form lifelong friendships, learn valuable life skills, and enjoy the sport in a safe and encouraging environment.

All of our coaches are volunteers who give up their own time to support, encourage, and develop every swimmer in the club. They are not paid for the countless hours they dedicate poolside, at competitions, and behind the scenes — they do it because they genuinely care about the swimmers and want to help each individual achieve their potential.

Our club values include:
- Respect
- Commitment
- Teamwork
- Sportsmanship
- Personal Excellence

## About Us
*Sub-menus: Our Squads, Membership, Our Facilities, Join Us, Codes of Conduct*

Torquay Leander Swimming Club is a friendly, ambitious, and community-focused swimming club based in the heart of Torquay. We are dedicated to helping swimmers of all ages and abilities achieve their full potential, whether they are swimming for fitness, personal development, or competitive success.

With a strong emphasis on quality coaching, athlete development, and team spirit, we provide a safe and supportive environment where swimmers can grow in confidence both in and out of the pool. Our structured training pathway allows swimmers to progress at their own pace, supported by experienced coaches who are passionate about developing technique, performance, and a lifelong love of swimming.

At Torquay Leander, we pride ourselves on being more than just a swimming club. We are a welcoming community where friendships are built, achievements are celebrated, and every swimmer is encouraged to be the very best version of themselves.

### Our Squads

**Putting our Swimmers First!**

At Torquay Leander Swimming Club, we provide a clear and supportive progression pathway for swimmers at every stage of their development. We recognise that every swimmer progresses at their own pace, and our squad structure is designed around ability and development — not simply the number of training hours completed.

Swimmers are placed into squads that best match their current skill level, allowing them to train in an environment where they can progress confidently and effectively. Our structured training programmes are carefully tailored to each squad, ensuring swimmers receive the right balance of technique work, endurance training, and race preparation at the appropriate intensity.

#### Development Squad
Ideal for swimmers who are new to club swimming from Stage 5 and above. This squad focuses on developing core swimming techniques, building stamina, and growing confidence within a competitive swimming environment.

#### Junior Squad
Designed for swimmers beginning their competitive journey, including those preparing for events such as County Championships. Training sessions are structured around improving race skills, endurance, technique, and overall performance in the pool.

#### Senior Squad
Our highest-level training squad for swimmers competing at County, Regional, and National level competitions. Training plans are specifically tailored around upcoming gala events and individual swimmer development, providing focused preparation and performance-driven coaching.

### Our Facilities

Our training venue is operated by Swim Torquay at Plainmoor Community Pool, providing a high-quality environment for both training and competition. The facility features a 4-lane swimming pool with a dedicated spectator viewing area, alongside male, female, and accessible changing facilities.

We are proud to be the only swimming club in Torbay with permanent diving blocks installed on every lane for regular training use, giving our swimmers valuable experience with competition-standard starts. The pool is also equipped with full race-specification anti-wave lane ropes for gala and competition events.

For the convenience of our members and families, the venue also benefits from free on-site parking, subject to vehicle registration being entered into the parking system tablet.

As a non-profit swimming club, we are committed to continually reinvesting back into the club, our swimmers, and our training environment. Our equipment includes:

- FINA-approved backstroke ledges
- Colorado Timing System
- Competition-standard anti-wave lane ropes
- Underwater filming equipment
- Video analysis technology for swimmer performance review

## Safeguarding & Welfare Support

Creating a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment for all children and young people is a priority within aquatics.

### Swimline – Confidential Support Service

Swim England works in partnership with the NSPCC to provide Swimline, a confidential freephone helpline for anyone involved in aquatic sports.

Swimline is available to both adults and children who may be worried about:
- Bullying
- Neglect
- Abuse
- Inappropriate behaviour
- Feeling unsafe or frightened
- Any safeguarding concern involving a child or young person

Even if you are unsure whether your concern is serious, you are encouraged to seek advice.

**How Swimline Works**

When you contact Swimline, you will reach a confidential answerphone service. You can leave:
- Your name
- Contact details
- Brief information about your concern (optional)

A member of the Swim England Safeguarding Team or a trained Swimline volunteer will return your call to discuss the concern and help decide on the most appropriate course of action.

**Response Times**
- Messages are normally returned on the next working day
- Messages left after 3pm on Fridays or over weekends will be returned on Monday
- If urgent support is needed, callers can choose to be transferred directly to the NSPCC for immediate advice from a trained counsellor

**Important Information**
- Calls to Swimline are free of charge
- Calls made from landlines will not appear on itemised bills
- Mobile phone providers may record outgoing calls on billing statements
- Where safeguarding concerns are identified, Swim England will always take appropriate action to help protect children and young people
