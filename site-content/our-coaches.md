## Page Content

OUR COACHES

Torquay Leander is run by a large number of volunteers who give up their free time for the love of swimming. Meet a number of our coaches and helpers that enable the club to thrive.

Our club also relies on volunteers who do many different roles on the committee and organising events for the club, if you are interested in helping us in any shape or form, please do get in touch. There is something for everyone.
