ANNUAL CLUB CHAMPIONSHIPS 2025

4th Nov, 10th Nov, 16th Nov, 20th Nov

This year Torquay Leanders’ Annual Club Championships will be a licenced competition. This is great news for the swimmers as it means all times achieved will be viewed as ‘official’ and can be used for other licenced meets and also for the ASA (Swim England) rankings. The licenced races will take place on the above dates, with some longer distance unlicenced races taking place at other times.

All Torquay Leander swimmers are capable of entering a race or two. The championships are fun and a great way to get to know other swimmers in the club. Speak to your coach if you are unsure about what you should enter.

DEVON CHAMPIONSHIP QUALIFYING TIMES 2026

The Devon Championship qualifying times are set annually by Devon County ASA and are published on their website. These “County Times” are required for entry into the Devon County Championships, which are typically held at the Plymouth Life Centre.

AWARDS NIGHT

Save the Date. Please join us on Friday 23rd January 2026 for our annual awards night. It’s great fun with food and a disco, a chance to celebrate all of our fantastic young swimmers. Trophies and medals won at the club championships will be awarded on this evening.
