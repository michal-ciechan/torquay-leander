## Page Content

MEMBERSHIP OF TORQUAY LEANDER

As a club that has been around for many years, we have had many generations of the same family return to swim at Torquay Leander because we pride ourselves on being a friendly, approachable, fun yet competitive swimming club.

We rely on our volunteers both coaches and parents, to do their bit whereever possible,  whether that is running the tuck shop (not during Covid unfortunately), volunteering on the committee, poolside helpers and selling raffle tickets being just a few, there is a job for everybody.

If you are interested in joining us either as a swimmer and would like to arrange a free trial please have a look at our membership fees below and get in touch.

How to join us

MEMBER BENEFITS

Being a member of Torquay Leander Swim Club comes with fantastic benefits, including exclusive discounts at a range of popular online retailers. This special offer allows our members to save on products they love, making it easier to access quality swim gear, sports equipment, and much more. By being part of our club, you not only gain access to top-notch coaching and a supportive community but also enjoy the added perk of these exclusive savings. It’s just one of the many ways we support our members both in and out of the pool!  The discount code information can be found here:  TL Discount Codes.

ANNUAL MEMBERSHIP FEES

Every swimmer at the club must be a member of Torquay Leander and also be a member of Swim England. This membership ensures insurance coverage and allows swimmers to compete and is managed by Torquay Leander – the annual club membership fee includes Swim England membership. The TL Club Membership fee is reviewed annually by the Finance Committee and the rate for the upcoming 12 months communicated to all Club members at the start of each calendar year. Annual membership fees are due by 31st January every year and any swimmer who has not renewed their membership by that date will not be permitted to swim.

For a swimmer that transfers from another swimming club to TL during the course of 12 months and already has Swim England membership, a reduced TL membership fee will be payable upon joining. They will then be required to renew their Swim England Membership via TL in the following January in line with this policy.

For a swimmer that joins TL after 1st October in any given year, the annual membership fee will be reduced.

MONTHLY SWIMMING FEES

Monthly subscription payments must be made by direct debit set up via Club Organiser. This ensures consistency and simplifies the exercise of cross checking payment. Monthly subscriptions are based on the number of hours swum per week and are reviewed annually by the Finance Committee.

These monthly subscriptions represent the annual subscription charge broken down into 12 equal monthly installments and as such are due for every month of membership, regardless of holiday, illness or any other short term absence, interruptions to regular sessions as a result of galas and annual club shutdown periods at Christmas and during the summer.
