## Page Content

TRAINING SESSIONS

All swimmers should be poolside 5 minutes before their session starts in order to start warming up.

Development Squad/Improvers

Our improving swimmers in the Development squad are working on reinforcing their basic skills, stroke technique and improving their dives and turns. An introduction to competitive swimming as their stamina improves, includes in-house ribbon galas and club championships before attending galas further afield.

Junior Squad

Swimmers continue to build their stamina through tougher training sessions. Race skills are developed such as starts, turns and finishes for each stroke and Individual Medley. Swimmers can enjoy competing in local competitions, team gala events and Devon Developments.

Senior Squad

Senior swimmers train with a commitment to participate in County competitions, team gala events and open meets. Those who swim for fitness only, are also encouraged to support the club in team galas.

TRAINING TIMETABLE

All swimmers have an allocated swimming day and time to ensure that swimming sessions do not get congested. Please note that movement between sessions is at the discretion of the Coach and must be discussed.
