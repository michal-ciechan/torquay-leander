## Page Content

WELCOME TO TORQUAY LEANDER SWIMMING CLUB

We aim to instill a life-long love of swimming and to build a program of excellence in training, team unity, character development and family support where every swimmer from every background has the opportunity to achieve their potential and compete at the highest level.

ABOUT US

Torquay Leander is a non-profit making organisation and SwimMark registered club.  As a club we are dedicated to the improvement and progression of all our swimmers including all ages and social backgrounds.

We aim to develop and encourage good sportsmanship, team spirit and provide an environment in which swimmers can realise their full potential.

Torquay Leander trains at Swim Torquay swimming pool, Torquay.

OUR FACILITIES

HOW TO JOIN US

If you are interested in joining us and would like to arrange a free trial, please first visit our membership page, here you will find details of our annual membership and monthly swimming fees and how to contact us.
