About Us

A Club for the Whole Family

Torquay Leander is a non-profit, SwimMark registered swimming club where every swimmer — from first dip to competition squad — is made to feel welcome and valued.

We build not just great swimmers, but great people. Our coaches focus on confidence, character and community alongside technical excellence.

💧
Water Confidence

Building a safe, positive relationship with water from the very start.

🏅
Competition

Regular club and open meets for those who want to race and improve.

🤝
Teamwork

Strong bonds and friendships formed through shared goals in the pool.

👨‍🏫
Expert Coaching

Experienced, qualified coaches who care about every swimmer's journey.

Our Facilities

Swimming at Swim Torquay

Torquay Leander trains at Swim Torquay — a modern, accessible pool in central Torquay providing everything our swimmers need to develop and thrive.

From beginner sessions in the learner pool to high-performance training in competition lanes, the facility supports swimmers at every level of their journey.

🏊
Competition Lanes

Full-length pool with dedicated coaching and racing lanes.

📍
Torquay, Devon

Conveniently located and easy to get to for all families.

🕐
Flexible Schedule

Morning, evening and weekend sessions across the week.

👶
All Ages Welcome

Dedicated areas for young beginners through to elite squads.
