About Us

More Than Just Swimming

We are a non-profit SwimMark registered club dedicated to developing every swimmer's potential — building confidence, teamwork, and excellence in the water.

🏆
Excellence in Training Structured coaching programs for all ability levels
🤝
Team Spirit Encouraging good sportsmanship and camaraderie
👨‍👩‍👧
Family Support A welcoming community for swimmers and their families

What We Offer

Training Programs

From first-timers to competitive racers, we have a program tailored for every swimmer.

Beginners

Learn to Swim

Building water confidence and fundamental techniques for young swimmers aged 5 and up.

Intermediate

Development Squad

Improving stroke technique, fitness, and race awareness in a structured environment.

Advanced

Competition Squad

High performance training for club, county, and regional competition.

Adult

Masters Fitness

Enjoyable, goal-oriented sessions for adult swimmers of all abilities.

Our Facilities

Training at Swim Torquay

Torquay Leander trains at Swim Torquay swimming pool — a modern, well-equipped facility in the heart of Torquay, Devon, providing dedicated competition lanes and coaching infrastructure for all our squads.

🏊
Competition Pool Full-length competition lanes available for training and time trials
📍
Swim Torquay, Torquay, Devon Conveniently located and accessible for all club members
🕐
Multiple Sessions Weekly Morning, evening and weekend training slots for all squads
