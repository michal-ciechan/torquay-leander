About the Club

Built on Grit & Heart

Torquay Leander is a non-profit SwimMark registered swimming club dedicated to the improvement and progression of every swimmer. We train at Swim Torquay and are proud to be Swim England affiliated.

Our programme builds excellence in the water while developing character, sportsmanship and team unity both in and out of the pool.
