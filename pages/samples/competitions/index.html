Featured Competition

Devon Mini League

The Devon Mini League is a team gala competition organised through Devon County ASA, designed to give younger and newer competitive swimmers their first taste of racing against other clubs in a fun, team-focused environment.

Swimmers race over 25m and 50m distances, with all competitors being 16 or under. Galas are held at pools across Devon including venues such as Exmouth.

🏊 Race Format

Team galas where clubs race head-to-head. Points scored across all events contribute to a team total.

📏 Distances

A mix of 25m and 50m events covering all four strokes plus individual medley and relays.

🎂 Age Limit

Open to swimmers aged 16 and under. Perfect for newer competitive swimmers making their start.

📍 Venues

Held at pools across Devon — Exmouth has recently hosted. Locations rotate between clubs each season.

Competition Programme

Beyond the Mini League, Torquay Leander swimmers compete at a range of local, county and regional events throughout the year.

Our flagship junior team competition. A series of galas across the season against other Devon clubs — the competition we've won back-to-back. Run by Devon County ASA.

Competition Calendar

Typical Competition Season

Competitions run throughout the year. Here's a general overview of what to expect across the season.

🏆
Autumn / Winter Devon Mini League Rounds

Team gala rounds begin. Multiple rounds held at venues across Devon. All U16 swimmers can take part — great team atmosphere and a brilliant first competition experience.

🏊
Throughout Year Open Meets & Club Championships

Individual swimmers enter open meets around the region to chase personal bests and qualifying times. Our internal Club Championships are typically held mid-season.

🌅
Year-round — Sundays Plymouth 50m Sessions

Early Sunday morning travel to Plymouth for 2-hour sessions in a 50m pool. Ongoing through the competitive season — check with your coach for current dates. Full details on the Training page →

🥇
Spring Devon County Championships

The major individual championship for qualifying swimmers. Held over 50m, this is the premier event for our performance squad each season.

🏆
End of Season Devon Mini League Final

The concluding round where the Devon Mini League champions are crowned. Torquay Leander have taken the title two years running — we aim to make it three!
