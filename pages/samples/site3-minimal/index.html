About the Club

A Community Built Around Swimming

Torquay Leander is a non-profit making swimming club affiliated with Swim England. We aim to instil a lifelong love of swimming while building a programme of excellence in training, team unity, character development and family support.

We are dedicated to the improvement and progression of all our swimmers — from those just gaining water confidence to those competing at regional level.

  • ✓ Qualified and experienced coaching team
  • ✓ Structured squad system for all levels
  • ✓ Regular competitive opportunities
  • ✓ Supportive family community
  • ✓ Free trial session available
Our Facilities

Training at Swim Torquay

We train at Swim Torquay swimming pool — a modern, well-maintained facility in central Torquay providing everything our squads need to thrive.

  • ✓ Full-length competition pool with dedicated coaching lanes
  • ✓ Modern changing facilities for all swimmers
  • ✓ Multiple weekly sessions — morning, evening and weekends
  • ✓ Conveniently located in Torquay, Devon
  • ✓ Accessible for swimmers of all ages and abilities
